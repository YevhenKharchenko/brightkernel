How to Play: Solve Quantum Puzzles

So here's the deal with BrightKernel - it's basically quantum physics meets puzzle game, and trust me, it'll make your brain work in ways you didn't expect. Here's how you solve each puzzle:

  • Manipulate Kernels:

    Control particles (kernels) that represent different quantum states and move them across the grid.

  • Use Quantum Principles:

    Apply quantum mechanics principles like superposition (particles in multiple states) and entanglement (linked particles) to solve the puzzle.

  • Create Paths:

    Link the particles in the correct order to complete each puzzle and move to the next stage.

New principles of quantum mechanics are introduced as you advance through levels
Some puzzles have multiple possible solutions, but only one "bright" solution is correct
