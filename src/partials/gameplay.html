How to Play Magic Match: Match 3

Match 3 or more identical jewels in a row to crush them and clear the board. Complete objectives and unlock new levels filled with more challenging puzzles. It starts off pretty easy, but man, some of these later levels will really make you think.

  • Swap Jewels:

    Tap a jewel and swap it with an adjacent one to form a line of 3 or more matching jewels.

  • Complete Objectives:

    Every level has specific objectives. Complete them to move on to the next level!

  • Enjoy the Magic:

    Relax with the enchanting jewel effects and smooth gameplay.
